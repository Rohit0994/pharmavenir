Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PharmAvenir, our medicines, services, and policies. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us.

🛒 Orders & Purchasing

1. How can I place an order with PharmAvenir?

You can place an order through three channels: (a) our online portal at pharmavenir.com/order, (b) our mobile app "PharmAvenir Care", or (c) by calling our 24/7 hotline at +91-22-4567-8900. For B2B bulk orders, contact our sales team at sales@pharmavenir.com.

2. What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

For retail customers, there is no minimum. For B2B clients, MOQ varies by product — typically 1,000 units for tablets/capsules and 100 vials for injectables. Custom MOQ can be discussed for contract manufacturing.

3. Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we currently ship to 40+ countries across Asia, Africa, Europe, and the Americas. Shipping costs and delivery times depend on destination, product type, and regulatory requirements.

4. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), UPI (in India), bank transfers, letters of credit (for export orders), and corporate cheques. All transactions are encrypted and secure.

5. Do prices include taxes and duties?

Domestic India prices include GST. International prices are shown ex-works (EXW) by default; customs duties, import taxes, and VAT are the buyer's responsibility unless specified in the contract.

💊 Products & Quality

6. Are PharmAvenir products approved by regulators?

Yes. Our manufacturing facilities are certified by WHO-GMP, US FDA, EU-GMP, TGA (Australia), and PIC/S. Each product is registered with the relevant national drug authority before being marketed in that country.

7. Are PharmAvenir generics as effective as branded equivalents?

Absolutely. All our generics undergo bioequivalence studies that prove they deliver the same active ingredient at the same rate and to the same extent as the originator brand. They are therapeutically interchangeable.

8. How do I verify a product is authentic?

Every PharmAvenir pack carries a 2D QR code and a unique serial number. Scan the code via our app or visit pharmavenir.com/verify and enter the serial number to confirm authenticity, manufacture date, and expiry.

9. What is the typical shelf life of your products?

Most tablets and capsules have a shelf life of 24–36 months from manufacturing. Liquids, suspensions, and biologicals typically have 12–24 months. Always check the printed expiry date on the package.

10. How should medicines be stored?

Most products are stored below 25°C in a dry place, away from direct sunlight. Insulin, vaccines, and certain biologicals require refrigeration (2–8°C). Always follow the package leaflet for specific storage instructions.

📦 Shipping & Returns

11. How long does delivery take?

Within India: 2–4 business days for metro cities, 5–7 days for remote areas. International: 7–21 business days depending on country and customs clearance.

12. Can I return a medicine?

For consumer safety reasons, opened medicines cannot be returned. Unopened medicines may be returned within 7 days of delivery if there is a manufacturing defect, damage during transit, or wrong product shipped. Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days.

13. What if my package arrives damaged?

Take a photo of the damaged package and email it to support@pharmavenir.com within 48 hours. We will arrange a replacement or refund at no cost to you.

📝 Prescriptions & Medical Advice

14. Do I need a prescription to buy your medicines?

Yes — prescription drugs (Rx) require a valid prescription from a licensed physician. Over-the-counter (OTC) products like multivitamins and wellness items can be purchased without a prescription.

15. Can I get medical advice from PharmAvenir?

Our medical helpline (+91-22-4567-8911) is staffed by qualified pharmacists 24/7 for product-related questions. For diagnosis or treatment advice, please consult a registered medical professional. Through our app, you can also book a tele-consultation with a partner doctor.

16. What should I do if I experience a side effect?

Stop the medication if the reaction is severe and contact your physician immediately. Please also report the event to our pharmacovigilance team at pv@pharmavenir.com or via the "Report Side Effect" form in our app. Your reports help improve drug safety for everyone.

💖 Patient Assistance & Pricing

17. Who is eligible for the Patient Assistance Program (PAP)?

Patients with documented chronic illness (cancer, diabetes, heart disease, etc.) and limited income are eligible. You will need to submit a doctor's prescription, income proof, and an enrollment form. Once approved, eligible medicines are provided free or at subsidized rates.

18. Why are PharmAvenir medicines more affordable?

Our scale, vertically integrated supply chain, and commitment to ethical generics keep production costs low without compromising on quality. We pass these savings to patients.

🏢 Business & Partnerships

19. How can my pharmacy/hospital become a partner?

Visit pharmavenir.com/partners or email partnerships@pharmavenir.com with your business details. A representative will contact you within 2 business days to discuss credit terms, product range, and supply schedules.

20. Do you offer contract manufacturing for other brands?

Yes. We are one of India's leading CMOs with capacity for 6 billion tablets, 200 million capsules, and 50 million vials per year. Contact cmo@pharmavenir.com for a quote.

21. Can I license a PharmAvenir product for my country?

Yes — we actively out-license products. Email business@pharmavenir.com with your country, target therapeutic area, and company profile.

🤖 Chatbot & Digital Support

22. What is PharmaBot?

PharmaBot is our AI-powered virtual assistant available 24/7 on this website and in our mobile app. It can help you with order tracking, product information, dosage guidelines, store locators, and connecting you with a human agent when needed.

23. Is the chatbot a substitute for medical advice?

No. PharmaBot provides general product and service information only. For diagnosis, treatment, or personalized medical advice, always consult a registered physician.

24. In which languages is the chatbot available?

PharmaBot currently supports English, Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, French, and Swahili. More languages are coming soon.

25. Is my conversation with the chatbot private?

Yes. All chats are encrypted in transit, anonymized for analytics, and used only to improve our service. We never share personal health information with third parties without consent. See our Privacy Policy.
