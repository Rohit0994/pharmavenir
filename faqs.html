Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PharmAvenir's services, engagement models, and capabilities. Have a question we haven't answered? Get in touch.

🏢 About the Company

1. What does PharmAvenir do?

We are a pharmaceutical intelligence and analytics consultancy. We help biopharma and life-sciences companies make better decisions by providing competitive intelligence, advanced analytics, forecasting, and market research across the product lifecycle.

2. Where are you located?

Our office is in Kondapur, on Botanical Garden Road, in Greater Hyderabad, Telangana, India. We support clients globally and serve teams across multiple time zones.

3. What kind of clients do you typically work with?

We partner with top pharmaceutical companies, biotech firms, and life-sciences organizations — from large multinationals to growing specialty companies — across more than 10 markets.

4. How big is your team?

We have a 50+ member expert team with collective experience exceeding 15 years across pharma analytics, forecasting, intelligence, and research.

🛠️ Services & Capabilities

5. What core services do you offer?

Four primary capabilities: (a) Business Analytics, (b) Forecasting & Modeling, (c) Competitive Intelligence, and (d) Market Research. These can be delivered individually or combined into an integrated program.

6. Which therapeutic areas do you cover?

Our team has worked across more than 15 therapeutic areas including oncology, cardiovascular and metabolic, immunology, neurology and CNS, respiratory, infectious diseases, rare diseases, women's health, and specialty areas.

7. Do you build custom Power BI dashboards?

Yes. Dashboard design and development on Power BI is one of our core analytics offerings, including data integration from CRM, sales, and channel sources.

8. Can you build forecasts for pre-launch assets?

Absolutely. We build patient-based and analog-driven forecasting models for both pipeline and in-market products, with built-in scenario planning and sensitivity analysis.

9. Do you cover medical congresses?

Yes — we provide live and post-event coverage of major medical congresses, with curated insights, abstract analysis, and competitor positioning.

10. Can you conduct primary research with KOLs and physicians?

Yes. We design and execute qualitative and quantitative primary research, including KOL interviews, physician studies, and patient research, in compliance with industry standards.

🤝 Engagement & Process

11. What does a typical engagement look like?

We follow a three-stage process: Discovery & Scoping, Research & Modelling, and Deliver & Support. The exact shape depends on whether the work is project-based, retainer, or embedded-team.

12. How do I get started?

Reach out via our contact form, email info@pharmavenirs.com, or call +91 70073 82835. We will set up a discovery conversation to understand your objectives and propose an approach.

13. How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary by scope. A focused intelligence brief can be a few weeks, a full forecasting build typically runs 6–10 weeks, and ongoing trackers/dashboards are continuous engagements.

14. Do you offer retainer engagements?

Yes. Many clients prefer ongoing retainers for continuous landscape monitoring, periodic forecast refreshes, or as embedded extensions of their internal teams.

15. Can you operate as an extension of our internal team?

Yes. Our embedded-team model places dedicated analysts and researchers alongside your in-house team for sustained, multi-workstream support.

🌐 Reach & Support

16. Do you serve clients outside India?

Yes — we currently serve clients across more than 10 markets, including North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific.

17. What are your support hours?

We offer 24/5 (Monday through Friday) support and can align team coverage to your time zone for project-critical needs.

18. How do you handle data confidentiality?

We work under strict NDAs and information-security practices. Client data is handled with controlled access, secure storage, and clear retention/return policies tailored to each engagement.

💼 Working With Us

19. Are you hiring?

We are continually growing our team. Visit our Careers page to see open roles or send your CV to careers@pharmavenirs.com.

20. Do you offer internships or graduate opportunities?

From time to time, we open internships and entry-level roles in analytics, research, and consulting. Email careers@pharmavenirs.com to express interest.

🤖 Chatbot & Digital Support

21. What can the chatbot help with?

Our virtual assistant can answer questions about our services, capabilities, engagement models, locations, and how to start a conversation with our team.

22. Does the chatbot replace a human consultant?

No. The chatbot is here to point you in the right direction quickly. For project scoping or strategic discussions, our consultants will engage with you directly.

23. Is my conversation with the chatbot confidential?

Yes. Conversations are encrypted in transit and used only to improve service quality. We never share your data with third parties without consent. See our Privacy Policy.
