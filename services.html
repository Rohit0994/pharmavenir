Our Services

Beyond manufacturing — PharmAvenir is a full-spectrum healthcare partner offering R&D, contract production, regulatory support, distribution, and patient programs.

What We Offer

Customized pharmaceutical services for B2B partners, governments, and patients alike.

🏭

Contract Manufacturing (CMO)

End-to-end manufacturing services across solid orals, liquids, injectables, ointments, and biosimilars. WHO-GMP, US FDA, and EU-GMP approved facilities. Capacity: 6 billion tablets annually.

🔬

R&D and Formulation Development

Custom formulation, generic development, bioequivalence studies, analytical method development, and stability studies — all under one roof at our 3 R&D centers.

📋

Regulatory & Dossier Services

CTD/eCTD dossier preparation, registrations in 40+ markets, post-approval lifecycle management, pharmacovigilance, and regulatory consulting.

🚚

Global Distribution & Supply Chain

Cold-chain logistics, warehousing across 4 continents, last-mile delivery partnerships, and serialization-compliant track-and-trace systems.

💖

Patient Assistance Program (PAP)

Free or subsidized medicines for eligible patients with chronic illness. More than 2 million patients have benefited since 2017. Visit our PAP enrollment desk.

📱

Digital Pharmacy & Telehealth

The PharmAvenir Care app: medicine ordering, e-prescriptions, doctor consultations, lab booking, and 24/7 medical helpline — available in 6 languages.

🎓

Medical Education & Training

CME-accredited webinars, KOL roundtables, hospital staff training, and patient education programs in disease awareness, adherence, and lifestyle.

🌐

Out-Licensing & Partnerships

Strategic partnerships for product licensing, technology transfer, joint ventures, and co-marketing across emerging and developed markets.

🚑

Government & Tender Supplies

Trusted supplier to UNICEF, WHO, MSF, Global Fund, and 25+ government health programs. Specialty in essential medicines for low- and middle-income countries.

How We Work — Our Process

1. Consultation

Free 30-minute discovery call to understand your requirements — product, volume, market, and timelines.

2. Proposal & Agreement

We provide a tailored quote with timelines, regulatory pathways, and risk assessment within 5 business days.

3. Development & Validation

Formulation, BE studies, stability, and pilot batches managed by dedicated project managers.

4. Manufacturing & QA

Commercial production with batch release after extensive QC testing in our NABL-accredited labs.

5. Regulatory Submission

Dossier filing, query handling, and approval support across all target markets.

6. Launch & Lifecycle

Distribution, marketing support, post-marketing surveillance, and lifecycle extension strategies.
