OUR SERVICES

Pharma Intelligence Across the Lifecycle

From competitive intelligence and advanced analytics to forecasting and market research — comprehensive solutions designed for pharma and life-science decision-makers.

Four Core Capabilities

Each capability can stand alone or combine into an integrated, multi-disciplinary engagement.

📈

Business Analytics

Translate complex pharmaceutical data into strategic insight. Our analytics offerings center on integrated dashboards, brand performance reporting, and cross-functional market assessments — built to support commercial leadership.

  • Power BI dashboard design and development
  • Brand and portfolio analytics
  • CRM and sales-force data integration
  • Channel and customer-level reporting
  • Market opportunity assessments
🔮

Forecasting & Modeling

Robust forecasting for both pipeline assets and in-market products. We build flexible models that account for uncertainty and let your teams test strategic options with clarity.

  • Pipeline and pre-launch forecasts
  • In-market revenue and demand modeling
  • Scenario planning and sensitivity analysis
  • Patient-based and epidemiology-driven models
  • Lifecycle management strategy support
🕵️

Competitive Intelligence

A continuous, structured view of the competitive landscape. We combine primary research with structured monitoring across more than 15 therapeutic areas to surface what matters — when it matters.

  • KOL and expert interviews
  • Competitor profiling and benchmarking
  • Conference and congress coverage
  • Pipeline and clinical trial monitoring
  • Real-time landscape alerts and newsletters
🔍

Market Research

Decision-ready research on patients, physicians, and the broader stakeholder ecosystem. We design qualitative and quantitative studies that answer your specific commercial and clinical questions.

  • Patient journey mapping and insight studies
  • Physician and HCP research
  • KOL identification, mapping, and segmentation
  • Message testing and concept evaluation
  • Quantitative surveys and primary fieldwork

Engagement Models

Flexible structures tailored to your team's needs and timelines.

Project-Based

Defined scope and timeline for a specific deliverable — a forecast, a research study, or a dashboard build.

Retainer / Ongoing

Continuous intelligence support, periodic refreshes, and dedicated capacity for monitoring and updates.

Embedded Team

Analysts and researchers operating as an extension of your in-house team for sustained, multi-workstream programs.

Therapeutic Area Coverage

Oncology & Hematology
