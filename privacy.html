Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

PharmAvenir Pharmaceuticals Ltd. ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use and protect it.

2. Information We Collect

  • Identity data: name, date of birth, gender.
  • Contact data: email, phone, billing/shipping address.
  • Health data (with consent): prescription details, medical history relevant to medication use.
  • Transaction data: orders, payments, delivery records.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device, cookies, and usage analytics.
  • Communication data: chats with PharmaBot, emails, call recordings (with notice).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill orders.
  • Provide customer service and respond to inquiries.
  • Verify prescription authenticity and ensure safe dispensing.
  • Conduct pharmacovigilance and report adverse events to regulators.
  • Send service updates and (with consent) marketing communications.
  • Improve our services through anonymized analytics.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with:

  • Logistics partners — solely to deliver your order.
  • Payment processors — to securely complete transactions.
  • Regulatory authorities — when legally required (e.g., adverse event reporting).
  • Service providers under strict confidentiality agreements (cloud hosting, analytics).

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion.
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.
  • Request data portability.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@pharmavenir.com.

6. Cookies

We use essential, analytics, and (with consent) marketing cookies. You can manage preferences in your browser or via our cookie banner.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3), secure cloud infrastructure (ISO 27001 certified), strict access controls, and regular penetration testing to protect your data.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed for the purposes described or as required by law (e.g., 7 years for transaction records, 10 years for pharmacovigilance data).

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 16. Pediatric medicines are purchased by parents/guardians; we do not knowingly collect data directly from minors.

10. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own. We use Standard Contractual Clauses and similar safeguards to ensure equivalent protection.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Material changes will be communicated via email or a website banner.
