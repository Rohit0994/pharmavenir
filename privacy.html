Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Our Commitment

PharmAvenir respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or engage with our services, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

2. Information We Collect

  • Identity & contact data: name, work email, company, role, phone — when you submit a form or get in touch.
  • Project data: information you share during the scoping, execution, and delivery of an engagement.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser, device, and usage analytics collected via cookies.
  • Communication data: records of emails, calls, and chatbot conversations.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to inquiries and consultation requests.
  • Scope, plan, and deliver client engagements.
  • Provide service updates and (with consent) periodic insights.
  • Improve our website and services through anonymized analytics.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share information only with vetted service providers (such as cloud hosting and analytics) under strict confidentiality terms, or where required by law.

5. Your Rights

  • Access information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion.
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant authority.

To exercise these rights, write to info@pharmavenirs.com.

6. Cookies

Our website uses essential and analytics cookies. You can manage your cookie preferences via your browser settings.

7. Data Security

We use modern encryption in transit, secure cloud infrastructure, role-based access controls, and regular reviews of our security posture to protect personal and client data.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal and project data only as long as needed to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law and contract.

9. International Transfers

Some processing may occur outside your country of residence. Where this happens, we apply appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable law.

10. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Material changes will be communicated via our website or, where appropriate, by email.
